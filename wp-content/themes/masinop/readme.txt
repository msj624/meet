Thank you for downloading Masinop WordPress theme.

Changing the logo:
==================
1. Use the logo.psd at the /images folder.
2. Edit it using your favorite graphics application.
3. Save as background-sitename.jpg and upload it at the /images folder.

Changing the adsense code:
==========================
1. Open adsense.php.
3. Change the digits to your Google publisher ID.

Changing the 468x60 header banner ad sponsors:
=========================================
1. Open headerbanner.php.
2. Change the url and image source to your new banner file.

Changing the 125x125 banner ads sponsors:
=========================================
1. Open banners.php.
2. Change the url and image source to your new banner files.
